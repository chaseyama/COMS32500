Welcome to Study Abroad Bristol! Created for COMS32500 at the University of Bristol, our website's purpose is to provide guidance and reccomendations for students studying abroad in Bristol. Written by current UoB students and alumni, our articles are written to help answer some of the questions about life in Bristol that may not be covered in the traditional study abroad orientation.
